Publications

Research contributions and scholarly works

Research areas. My research focuses on computational immunology and data analysis approaches to understand immune system dynamics. I work on projects that combine experimental data with computational modeling to advance our understanding of immune responses.

Ongoing work. Currently, I am involved in research projects at the Allen Institute for Immunology that leverage cutting-edge computational methods to analyze immune system data. This work contributes to our broader understanding of immune function and has potential applications for therapeutic development.

Future publications. I am actively working on manuscripts that will be submitted to peer-reviewed journals. These publications will showcase the innovative research being conducted at the Allen Institute and my contributions to the field of computational immunology.
